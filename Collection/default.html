Collection Evaluation and Lecture Services

We believe that something is better than nothing.  Therefore, documenting the archaeological record of the Chesapeake Bay region is very important.  We have examined thousands of artifacts found at numerous archaeological sites.  If you have a collection, we are willing to evaluate your collection and give you an idea about its cultural affiliation and age.  Contact us (darrinlowery@yahoo.com) if you want your collection evaluated.
We have given over 100 public lectures or presentations on various topics associated with the geology and archaeology of the Chesapeake Bay region.  We are willing to cater a lecture to your specific interests.  Contact us at darrinlowery@yahoo.com if you’re interested in a presentation.
